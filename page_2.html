Maize & Blue Cupboard

An equitable access to healthy, nutritious, and nourishing food and the ability to prepare it for themselves or others.

What we do

The Maize and Blue Cupboard is here to provide an immediate and comprehensive response for the U-M community that are experiencing food insecurity at alarming rates.
